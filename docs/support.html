Booster Countdown Support

Booster Countdown is an independent launch-tracking app for iPhone and iPad.

Contact

For help, feature requests, or bug reports: Open a support issue

Common fixes

  • Pull to refresh on Launches or History pages after opening the app.
  • If data appears stale, close and reopen the app, then refresh again.
  • If premium features do not appear, use Restore in Settings.
  • If widgets show old/empty data, open the app once, refresh, then wait a minute for widget reload.

Before contacting support

Please include:

  • Device model (for example, iPhone 14 Pro or iPad Air 5).
  • iOS/iPadOS version.
  • App version.
  • Exact steps to reproduce the issue.
  • Screenshots if possible.
