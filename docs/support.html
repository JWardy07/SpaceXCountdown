Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the next launch time differ between app and widget?

The widget refreshes on a timeline and may lag behind the app for a short period. Open the app and refresh launches to force the latest schedule.

Why does Launches show no upcoming missions?

This usually means the data provider has no currently scheduled launches or a temporary API/network issue occurred. Use Retry and check again after a few minutes.

Why is a launch missing from History?

History is sourced from public launch APIs and can take time to synchronize after events. Pull to refresh and check back shortly for newly published records.

How do I report a bug?

Email support with these details: app version, iOS version, device model, screenshots, and exact steps to reproduce.
