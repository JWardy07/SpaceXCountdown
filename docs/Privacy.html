Privacy Policy

Booster Countdown

1. Who we are

Booster Countdown is an independent launch-tracking app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any launch provider.

2. Data we collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email, contacts, photos, or precise location.

Data stored on your device may include:

  • Cached launch and satellite feed data for offline viewing/performance.
  • Your app preferences (theme, notification settings, refresh choices).
  • Premium entitlement state (whether premium is unlocked).

3. In-App Purchases

Payments are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, local reminders may be scheduled on your device for launch events.

5. Third-party data and links

The app retrieves publicly available aerospace data from third-party providers and may open external links (for example, streams or articles). Those third-party services have their own privacy policies.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your data and we do not share personal data with advertisers.

7. Your choices

  • Clear app data by deleting the app.
